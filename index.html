About Me

I discovered coding at the age of 8, when I started creating websites in HTML. From then on, I knew I was cut out to work in IT. Then, at the age of 15, I started learning Python, C and C++ in my spare time. I was passionate about programming (and still am today)!

Then, in 2021, I started digging into blockchain development. I learned how blockchains work, Solidity and so on. That's when I landed my first tech jobs.

Later, in 2022, I realized the potential of artificial intelligence and that this technology would have a major impact on our world. So I started learning more about data science, deep learning and the mathematics behind it.
